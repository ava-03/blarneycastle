Blarney Castle and Gardens Mobile App Privacy Policy

This mobile application does not collect personal user data.

Location Data

If location permission is granted, your location is used only on your device to show your position on the map. Location information is never stored or transmitted to our servers.

Server Requests

The app connects to our server to retrieve public visitor information such as queue times and opening hours. No personally identifiable information is transmitted.

Analytics & Tracking

This app does not use advertising frameworks, tracking, or third-party analytics.
